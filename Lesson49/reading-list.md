# Reading list

## JS

- [You Don't Know JS](https://github.com/getify/You-Dont-Know-JS)
- [Eloquent JavaScript](https://eloquentjavascript.net/)

## General

- [High Performance Browser Networking](https://hpbn.co/) — Ilya Grigorik; how the web actually works
- [Google's Engineering Practices](https://google.github.io/eng-practices/) — free; aligns with *Software Engineering at Google*
- *The Pragmatic Programmer* — David Thomas and Andrew Hunt
- *Software Engineering at Google*
- *Clean Code* — Robert C. Martin
- *Designing Data-Intensive Applications* — Martin Kleppmann; databases, queues, consistency, scaling
- *Fundamentals of Software Architecture* — Richards & Ford; bridge from dev to architect/lead thinking
- *The Manager's Path* — Camille Fournier; how leads and managers think (useful even if you stay IC)
